Bad Auntie’s was
raised by a village.

Teresa always had a way of bringing people together. Raised in a close-knit community, she learned early that connection isn't formal. It's instinctive. As she grew through business roles, she was preparing, long before she knew for what. Then it happened. At a restaurant. In margarita form. A prickly pear margarita so good it demanded further exploration.

Teresa went home determined to reimagine it, to see if she could make it even better. She did. Soon, she was sharing her mix with friends, and the reaction was always the same: one sip, then:

“This is so good!”

You need to bottle this up!

That was the moment.
Every lesson. Every auntie who made room without asking. It all led here. Bad Auntie's MULTIMIX is Bold. Welcoming. Full of flavor. And absolutely meant to be shared. Bad Auntie didn't just bring the drink. She brought the vibes. Now in a bottle.
